GMROI Methodology & Definitions
📐
GMROI = Gross Margin ÷ Average Inventory at Cost. Measures how many dollars of gross margin are returned for every dollar invested in inventory.
📐
Avg Inventory at Cost = Average Daily Units on Hand × Average Unit Cost (COGS ÷ Units Sold). Derived from daily FACT_INVENTORY_SNAPSHOT data across FY2026.
📐
Inventory Turns = COGS ÷ Average Inventory at Cost. Higher turns = faster inventory cycling.
💡
