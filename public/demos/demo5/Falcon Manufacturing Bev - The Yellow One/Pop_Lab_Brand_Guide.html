05 ★ Quick Reference
BRAND RULES
Rule #1
Black background is the default. Always. White text on black.
Rule #2
#4CE0D2 Pop Turquoise is the ONE accent color for CTAs, highlights, and positive indicators.
Rule #3
#FF1654 Pop Pink is for alerts and negative values only. Use sparingly.
Rule #4
Typography is BOLD, CONDENSED, ALL CAPS. Like a concert poster or skateboard graphic.
