Semiconductor pipeline convention: A design-win is a socket committed at a customer that will convert to future revenue. 7-stage funnel: Prospect → Engage → Eval → Design → Qual → Prod → EOL. Pipeline value = annual unit estimate × base ASP. Weighted = value × win probability.
