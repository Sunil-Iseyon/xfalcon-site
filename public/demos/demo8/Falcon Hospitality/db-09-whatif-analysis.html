Model Assumptions & Methodology

  • Per-unit economics derived from all-time actuals across 75 locations (20 hotels, 22 Ember & Oak, 18 Grand Slice, 15 Whisk & Co.)
  • Hotel revenue model: Rooms x 365 days x Occupancy % x ADR, plus ancillary revenue at historical ratios (F&B 15.7%, Spa 5.4%, Ancillary 11.4%)
  • Restaurant revenue: Based on per-location historical averages with seating capacity scaling and ramp-up factor
  • Menu item impact: Estimated as adoption rate % x total existing transactions x average item price, spread across all restaurant/bakery locations
  • Labor costs: Scaled proportionally per new location based on brand-level per-location historical costs
  • COGS margins: Food 32%, Beverage 30%, Alcohol 22%, Room 20% (from actual data)
  • Waste reduction savings applied to historical waste cost per location
  • Price adjustment applied uniformly across all revenue streams for existing portfolio
