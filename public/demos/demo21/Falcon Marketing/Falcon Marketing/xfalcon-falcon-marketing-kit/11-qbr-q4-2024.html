Executive Summary

Q4 2024 closed agency revenue at $6.45B, up 1.9% over Q4 2023, with November the strongest month. Direct Mail and Connected TV remained the two largest spend channels but Out-of-Home delivered the highest ROAS at 4.63x. Production turnaround held steady at 15.6 days with on-spec at 92.0%. Margin softened 0.1pp to 42.9% — within tolerance and consistent with the rate-card stability across all 15 service lines. Vertex Financial Group, Meridian Health, and HorizonTech together accounted for 47.6% of agency-wide billings, a 0.3pp concentration uptick worth flagging for risk discussions.
